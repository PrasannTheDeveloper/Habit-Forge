⚙️ What's Inside

BUILT FOR
REAL GROWTH

📅
Weekly Grid View

See all your habits across the week at a glance. Track completions, values, and rest days in a clean, dense grid.

🔥
Streak Tracking

Current and best streaks per habit. Rest days don't break your streak — they're part of your strategy.

📊
Consistency Rate

An honest score: required days minus missed days, expressed as a percentage. No fluff, just your real performance.

🗺️
Heat Map

GitHub-style activity heatmap lets you spot patterns, slumps, and winning streaks at a bird's-eye view.

📓
Growth Journal

Log your wins, challenges, moods, and productivity scores. Link journal entries directly to specific habits.

☁️
Google Drive Backup

One-click backup and restore via Google Drive. Your data, stored in your own account — always under your control.

🏷️
Categories

Organise habits into custom categories. Health, Career, Learning, Mindfulness — you define the structure.

😴
Rest Day Planning

Set weekly rest days or specific dates per habit. Recovery is part of the forge — not a failure of it.

🔐
Google Sign-in

Secure, frictionless authentication with your Google account. Your data stays local in AppData — no cloud lock-in.

📋 Habit Types

TRACK IT
YOUR WAY

Not every habit is a simple yes/no. HabitForge supports five tracking modes so every habit fits naturally.

Checkbox Numeric Hours Percent Checklist
Checkbox

Simple did-it-or-not. Perfect for binary habits like meditation, journaling, or cold showers.

Numeric

Log a number — glasses of water, push-ups, pages read. Set a goal and track progress daily.

Hours

Track time spent. Ideal for deep work, study sessions, or sleep tracking.

Checklist

Define sub-tasks for a habit and mark each one. Great for morning routines or workout circuits.
