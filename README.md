## Overview

HabitForge is a free Windows desktop app that helps you build powerful habits, eliminate bad ones, and track your consistency over time. It goes beyond simple checkboxes — with a rank progression system, milestone goal ladder, consistency scoring, and a built-in growth journal, HabitForge treats your habits like a forge. The more heat you apply, the stronger you get.

## Features

### Habit Tracking
- Five habit types — **Checkbox, Numeric, Hours, Percent, List**
- Track habits you want to **Build** and habits you want to **Quit**
- Custom goal values per habit
- Weekly grid view with full navigation

### Streak & Rank System
- Automatic streak tracking with 15 rank tiers
- **Dormant → Spark → Ember → Molten → Forming → Stable → Solid → Shaped → Forged → Hardened → Refined → Tempered → Mythic → Legendary → Unbreakable**
- Best streak preserved separately from current streak
- Champion card highlighting your strongest active habit

### Consistency Engine
- Consistency rate calculated from required days vs missed days
- Goes beyond raw streaks — tracks actual performance over time

### Milestone Goal Ladder
- Progressive goals — **7, 14, 21, 30, 40, 50, 66, 90 days**
- Completion and fail counters per milestone
- Automatically advances to next rung on completion

### Rest Day Scheduling
- Set weekly recurring rest days per habit
- Add specific date exceptions
- Rest days never break your streak or hurt your consistency score

### Heatmap
- Visual history across weeks and months
- Instantly spot patterns, dry spells, and momentum

### Analytics
- Weekly and monthly performance analysis
- Trend tracking per habit over time

### Categories
- Organize habits into custom categories
- Keep your list clean as it grows

### Growth Journal
- Full progress diary with mood, productivity score, tags
- 17 mood options — Focused, Motivated, BurntOut, Reflective, and more
- Log your Win of the Day and Challenge of the Day
- Link entries to specific habits

### Backup & Restore
- Full data export and restore
- Your progress never gets lost

## Installation

### Microsoft Store
Search **HabitForge** on the Microsoft Store and install for free.

### Manual Install
1. Go to [Releases]([https://github.com/prasannthedeveloper/habitforge/releases](https://github.com/PrasannTheDeveloper/Habit-Forge/releases/tag/habittracker))
2. Download the latest `.msix` or setup file
3. Run the installer
4. Launch HabitForge and start forging

## Getting Started

1. **Create your first habit** — hit Create New, pick a habit type, set your goal
2. **Check in daily** — mark habits complete from the Weekly View
3. **Watch your rank climb** — streak builds your rank from Spark toward Unbreakable
4. **Set a milestone** — challenge yourself with the Goal Ladder
5. **Journal your journey** — log wins, struggles, and reflections in the Growth Journal
